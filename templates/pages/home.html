Anagram Hunt

This game challenges your ability to guess anagrams based on the word length you choose. You will be scored by the number of correct answers and you will be given a 60 minute timer to get as many correct answers in that time frame. Login is required to play. Good luck and enjoy the game!

Math Facts Practice

It is time to go back to school and check your math skills. This is a fun interactive game that lets you pick the max number length and operator. There is a 30 minute timer to get as many correct answers as possible to calculate your score. Login is required to play. Have fun!
